# VelociDice Rules

VelociDice uses Farkle-style scoring. On your turn, select dice that contribute to a valid score, then either hold and reroll or end the turn to bank your points.

## Goal

Score points by selecting scoring dice and banking them before you Farkle. The first player to reach the target score wins.

## How A Turn Works

1. Roll the dice.
2. Select dice that score points.
3. Choose one of two actions:
   - Hold and reroll to add the selected score to your round score and roll the remaining dice.
   - Bank and end turn to add your round score to your total score.
4. If you roll and have no possible scoring dice, you Farkle.
5. If you Farkle, your unbanked round score is lost and your turn ends.

## What The Scores Mean

- Total is your banked score. These points are safe.
- Round is your current unbanked turn score. These points are at risk.
- Selected is the score from the dice you currently have selected.

## Buttons

- Hold and Reroll: Adds the selected dice to your round score, then rolls the remaining active dice.
- Bank and End Turn: Adds your round score and selected score to your total, then passes the turn.
- Rules: Opens the scoring guide.
- End Game / New Game: Resets the game.

## Selecting Dice

Only select dice that contribute to the score. If you select a die that does not score, you cannot hold or bank until you deselect it.

Examples:

- 1 is valid because it scores 100.
- 5 is valid because it scores 50.
- 1, 5 is valid because both dice score.
- 1, 2 is not valid because the 2 does not score.
- 2, 2, 2 is valid because three 2s score 200.

## Farkle

A Farkle happens when the active dice have no possible scoring dice.

Example Farkle rolls:

- 2, 3, 4, 6
- 2, 2, 3, 3, 4, 6

When you Farkle:

- Your round score becomes 0.
- Your banked total score does not change.
- The only available action is to end the turn.

## Hot Dice

If all dice are held because they all scored, you get to roll all six dice again. Your round score stays active, so you can keep building points, but those points are still at risk until you bank them.

Example:

1. You select 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 for 1,500 points.
2. All dice scored, so all six dice are rolled again.
3. Your round score remains 1,500.
4. If the next roll Farkles, that 1,500 is lost.

## Example Turn

1. You roll 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 6.
2. You select the 1 for 100 points.
3. You choose Hold and Reroll.
4. Your round score becomes 100, and the remaining dice reroll.
5. You roll 5, 5, 2, 3, 4.
6. You select both 5s for 100 points.
7. You can bank 200 total round points, or risk another reroll.

## Turn Rules

- Select only dice that contribute to the score.
- Held dice add to the current round score.
- Ending the turn banks the round score into the current player's total.
- If a roll has no possible scoring dice, the player Farkles.
- After a Farkle, all unbanked round points are lost.
- After a Farkle, the only available action is to end the turn.
- If all dice are held, all six dice are rerolled and the current round score is kept.
- First player to the target score wins.

## Scoring Rules

| Dice | Score |
| --- | ---: |
| Single 1 | 100 |
| Single 5 | 50 |
| Three 1s | 1,000 |
| Three 2s | 200 |
| Three 3s | 300 |
| Three 4s | 400 |
| Three 5s | 500 |
| Three 6s | 600 |
| Three pairs | 750 |
| Two triplets | 2,500 |
| Five-die straight, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 or 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 | 1,000 |
| Six-die straight, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 | 1,500 |

## Four, Five, And Six Of A Kind

Four, five, and six of a kind are based on the three-of-a-kind score for that die face.

| Combo | Multiplier |
| --- | ---: |
| Four of a kind | Three-of-a-kind score x 2 |
| Five of a kind | Three-of-a-kind score x 3 |
| Six of a kind | Three-of-a-kind score x 4 |

Examples:

| Dice | Score |
| --- | ---: |
| 1, 1, 1, 1 | 2,000 |
| 1, 1, 1, 1, 1 | 3,000 |
| 1, 1, 1, 1, 1, 1 | 4,000 |
| 2, 2, 2, 2 | 400 |
| 2, 2, 2, 2, 2 | 600 |
| 2, 2, 2, 2, 2, 2 | 800 |

## Five-Die Straight With Leftover Die

A five-die straight uses five dice. If a sixth selected die is also a scoring die, it can be added to the straight score.

| Dice | Score | Valid Selection |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 | 1,000 | Yes |
| 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 | 1,000 | Yes |
| 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 1 | 1,100 | Yes |
| 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 5 | 1,050 | Yes |
| 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 2 | 1,000 | No, because the extra 2 does not score |
